The Profits Must Flow

Jim was like every child. He’d attend school then come home and sit in his chair and drink his favourite drink for hours on end. This drink was appealing to all ages. Of course it’d be so. The drink manufacturers spent tremendous amounts of money ensuring their drink was the most sought after on the market. Eventually the harm that these drinks were causing to society as a whole was noted by the government. They realized that they must keep children safe from the vile drinks. Under scrutiny it was noted that children always were sitting when consuming these drinks. With this observation in hand they went to authoring legislation. These new bills got pretty names and set out goals to “keep children safe”, but there was a common relation between these bills. They only banned chairs for children. If an adult wanted a chair they must present their government identification. Now this may seem like a sensible compromise, but as you dear reader likely know children are not as stupid as we paint them. They discovered they can just sit on another flat object and drink the concoction they desired. Governments watched in horror as youth continued to consume the poison sitting on tables, countertops, floors, and steps. Rapidly new laws came out that controlled “horizontally aligned surfaces”. To even own “horizontally aligned surfaces” you needed a permit and to provide your ID. Placing a coffee down on your legally purchased “coffee horizontally aligned surface” you hear the faint echoes of “please scan your ID” in the halls. Throughout all of this experts in the health and food fields demanded laws to regulate what was put in these nasty vials. For they knew that these drinks were harmful to everyone of all ages. Not that you or anyone else would’ve heard them given their calls were muffled by the money these companies amassed. They were easily able to control the narrative either directly through ensuring readers only read what they wanted them to see, or indirectly by lobbying those who made the laws. For it was known to these corporations that changing how they operate would only reduce the amount of profit they’d receive. To appease shareholders and their wealthy management they could not allow anything that would stifle their upward growth. So say good bye to your free access and ownership of chairs, tables, shelves, countertops, and floors. As there is only one solution to consumption of a toxic substance, Government oversight on your access of “horizontally aligned surfaces”. Now as you scan your face to sit and drink your favourite flavour you know certainly the children are protected from the tonic.
